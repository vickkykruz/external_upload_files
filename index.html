About The Association

The Dean of school of science, Mr Olusanya appointed Mr Samuel from computer science department as the staff adviser and then appointed the first caretaker committee of SESA now NASS on 27th of November, 2023. The committee was led by Comr. Shonibare Ayomide(NACOS President) who was the chairman of the committee and also Comr. Ogundele Ezekiel from Mathematics department who was the secretary. Other members include Fisuyi Tolulope from computer science department, Atanda Islamiyah Abisola from Health Education department, Adekoya Oluwabunmi Glory from Biology department, Fatukasi Janet from Chemistry department and Adeyemi Muyideen from Physics department. For equity and transparency, the dean appointed the committee across all the department.
